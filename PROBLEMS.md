| Messaging | Fan-out, retry, deduplication, durable delivery, multi-channel |

### Difficulty Guide

* **Easy**. Warm-up. Solvable in a 30 to 45 minute interview slot.
* **Medium**. Standard onsite question. Has at least one non-obvious trade-off.
* **Hard**. Senior-level. Multiple right answers; you must defend yours.
